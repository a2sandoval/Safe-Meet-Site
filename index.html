SafeMeet is a mobile application that provides a safe in-person meetup experience!

In 2019, our founders realized that in-person meetups could become safer and more accountable.

They realized that you never really knew who was going to show up, or if this stranger would even make it on time!

These situations proved frustrating, dissapointing, and downright scary.

That's why SafeMeet was born.

SafeMeet provides an extensive user verification process when compared to what’s available today.

With the SafeMeet app you can view a history of all your meetups, an in-app messaging center, an ETA dashboard, Covid-19 guidelines, and more.

SafeMeet FAQ's

SafeMeet a website or mobile app?
SafeMeet's users will be able to download our mobile app for Android and iOS platforms. We currently have a website, which serves as an informational front regarding our app and as a way for all interested parties to contact us.

How does SafeMeet exactly work?

User account verification happens from the moment a new user downloads SafeMeet and sign-ups, thus creating a community of users who can trust each other. SafeMeet users will also be able to communicate via the app's messaging platform for all meet-up related questions or concerns. And during the actual meet-up, users will be able to view ETA and know if the other party is actually on their way, has arrived, or is actually not going at all. Upon arrival, parties involved can complete the meet-up transaction via a cashless process, providing for an easier and safer method of payment.

Will SafeMeet be a free ?

SafeMeet's app will be free to download on iOS and Android platforms. However; a per transaction fee will apply to all parties using out cashless payment option. A $1 per meet-up will also apply to all completed meet-up transactions. These applicable fees will ensure SafeMeet's services maintain quality and continue improving into the future.

Where and when can I use SafeMeet?

The app will be available in the greater Bay Area upon initial launch. Our goal is to expand to all cities across the United States and beyond. The app is still under construction and the launch/release date is yet TBD.

Who can I contact if I'd like to invest, have a question, and or have a suggestion?

You can contact us directly via the contact form provided on our website. We will respond promptly. We look forward to your questions, suggestions, and or interest for investment.
